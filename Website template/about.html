OUR START

Founded in 1995, Build was started by two brothers who also happened to be self-employed contractors. Greg and John worked for years separately and together on a variety of projects. Collectively, they gained experience in every area of home renovation: plumbing, electrical, structural, and aesthetic. Build is aptly named for the work that went into their experience and the company.

OUR TEAM - 1/2 COLUMN

Over 20 years our team has grown immensely to meet the growing demand for residential and commercial contractors. We employ only the best engineers, designers, and specialists to ensure that each project is guaranteed the expertise it deserves. Over the years we have also developed a strong network of connections with other professionals in the field so there are no restrictions on what we can provide.

 

OUR PHILOSOPHY - 2/2 COLUMN

Each project is entered into as a partnership. Our number one concern is to deliver what you dream of, as long as it is structurally sound and safe. We will never dissuade you from what you desire simply because it is more work for us, and we are always happy to do the research needed to create something new and innovative.

 

DESIGNERS - 1/3 COLUMN

Each member of our design team is trained and experienced. We have a combination of interior and exterior-focused designers.

 

SPECIALISTS - 3/3 COLUMN

With the breadth of services we offer, we have the opportunity to employ a variety of specialists working within the renovation industry.

 

 

ENGINEERS - 2/3 COLUMN

This team guarantees the safety of any of our renovations. With required accreditation and experience, they deliver efficient solutions.
